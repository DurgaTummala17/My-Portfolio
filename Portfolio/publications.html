Publications

The Uncharted Glory

Embarking on the maiden voyage of my design endeavors, I am delighted to unveil 'The Uncharted Glory,' my inaugural magazine creation. With fervor and dedication, I crafted this publication as a testament to my burgeoning creative spirit. Leveraging the versatile tools of Canva, I collaborated with the Rotaract Club of Pvp Siddhartha to bring this vision to life. 'The Uncharted Glory' captures the essence of Youth Day, radiating an electrifying fusion of inspiration and celebration. Join me on this enthralling expedition as we traverse the vibrant landscape of India's youthful energy and aspirations.

Telugu Tarangam

Excited to introduce my second venture into magazine design, 'Telugu Tarangam'! As a collaboration with the Rotaract Club of PVP Siddhartha, this cultural magazine serves as a vibrant showcase of Andhra Pradesh's rich traditions and stories. With meticulous attention to detail, each page of 'Telugu Tarangam' is a celebration of our state's diverse heritage, blending history, traditions, and contemporary narratives. Join me on this cultural journey as we proudly share the beauty and pride of Andhra Pradesh with the world through the colorful pages of 'Telugu Tarangam'.
